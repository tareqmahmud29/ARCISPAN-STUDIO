Frequently Asked Questions

Any questions? You'll find the answers here.

Why should I choose Arcispan Studio for my project? +

Our service is aimed at self-employed individuals, small entrepreneurs, and medium-sized companies who need professionally created architectural solutions but want them created externally due to lack of time or employee capacities. For us, quality and good service don't have to be expensive.

How can I prepare for the first consultation? +

It's helpful to gather any inspiration images, know your budget range, and have a list of must-have features. If you have existing property plans or surveys, those are also useful.

How long does a typical project take? +

The timeline varies depending on project scope and complexity. A small residential project might take 3-6 months from design to completion, while larger commercial projects can take a year or more. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Can you work with existing structures? +

Absolutely! We frequently work on renovations, extensions, and remodeling projects. We can assess your existing structure and provide solutions that enhance or transform the space.
